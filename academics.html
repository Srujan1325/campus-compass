Academic Excellence

Deep dive into curriculum, rules, and tips to succeed.

📘 Grading System & CGPA

Amrita follows a relative grading system (in most courses). Your grade depends on the class average. A CGPA above 8.5 is considered excellent for placements. Consistent effort in internal assignments and continuous evaluation makes finals much easier.

⚙️ Core Branches & Programs

Computer Science (CSE): Focuses on software engineering, algorithms, and full-stack development.
CSE (Artificial Intelligence): Specialized track focusing on Machine Learning, Neural Networks, and Data Science.
Computer and Communication Engineering (CCE): Integration of computer science with communication networks and telecommunications.
Master of Business Administration (MBA): Premium management program focusing on leadership, business analytics, and marketing.

👨‍🏫 Mentorship Program

Every fresher is assigned a faculty mentor. Meet them regularly—they can guide you on internships, research papers, and electives.
