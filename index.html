Academic Life

Everything you need to know to stay ahead in your classes.

📚 Branches & Structure

Amaravati offers cutting-edge B.Tech programs in CSE, CSE (AI), and ECE. Classes blend theory with hands-on lab sessions.

⏱️ Attendance Rules

75% is mandatory. Don't take it lightly! Falling below means you can't write end-semester exams (even for medical reasons, you need at least 65% with a certificate).

📝 Exam Pattern

Continuous Evaluation (50%) + End Semester (50%). Periodicals happen twice a semester. Consistent studying is the key!

💡 Pro CGPA Tips

Score well in internal labs and assignments—they are easy marks.

Freshers Survival Kit

✅ The Do's

  • Sit in the first few rows (you'll focus better).
  • Ask questions. Professors at Amrita are very approachable.
  • Start learning to code early, outside the syllabus.
  • Maintain a healthy sleep schedule.

❌ The Don'ts

  • Don't wait until the night before the exam to study.
  • Don't skip labs. They carry heavy weightage.
  • Don't stay in your room all day. Socialize!
  • Don't ignore emails from the administration.

⏳ Time Management

Use the "Pomodoro Technique" for studying. Block out 2 hours every evening just for self-learning and coding practice.
