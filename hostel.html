Life at Amaravati

Your home away from home.

🛏️ Hostel Rooms & Facilities

Rooms are typically 4-sharing for freshers. Each student is provided with a cot, study table, chair, and a personal cupboard. Rooms are well-ventilated, and cleaning staff sweeps and mops the corridors and rooms regularly. High-speed Wi-Fi is available 24/7 (some gaming/streaming sites may be restricted during class hours).

🍛 Mess & Specific Timings

The mess serves wholesome pure vegetarian meals (South Indian & North Indian mix).
Timings:
• Breakfast: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
• Lunch: 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM (Staggered by batches)
• Evening Snacks: 5:15 PM - 5:45 PM
• Dinner: 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM
Night cafeterias are available until midnight for late-night cravings!

🧺 Laundry & Utilities

Washing machines are not provided in individual rooms, but there is a dedicated campus laundry service where you can drop off your clothes (paid per piece or by weight). Filtered drinking water coolers are available on every floor of the hostel.

🔒 Biometric Rules & Safety

Attendance is strictly monitored. You must punch in your biometric attendance at the hostel gate before the curfew time (8:30 PM for all students). The campus is highly secure with 24/7 security guards and CCTV cameras everywhere.

🎟️ The Outpass System

Leaving the campus requires an approved "Outpass". You must apply via the student portal (AUMS). Day passes are for local shopping/outings on weekends, and Home passes are for visiting family. Wardens will call your parents to verify home passes!

🏀 Sports & Recreation

Extensive sports grounds for Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, and Badminton. Evening matches between branches and hostels are a major part of the culture here. A gymnasium is also available for fitness enthusiasts.

Important Rules

🏛️ Campus Rules

  • Dress Code: Students must wear smart casuals or formal attire. Shorts, sleeveless shirts, and highly distressed jeans are not allowed in academic blocks.
  • ID Cards: ID cards must be worn around the neck at all times while inside the campus and academic buildings.
  • Mobile Phones: Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited inside classrooms and laboratories during instruction hours.
  • Discipline: Any form of ragging is strictly prohibited and is a punishable offense leading to immediate expulsion.
  • Vehicles: Day scholars are allowed to bring vehicles but must park in designated areas. Hostellers cannot keep personal vehicles on campus.

🏠 Hostel Rules

  • Curfew: All hostellers must punch in before the designated curfew time of 8:30 PM.
  • Quiet Hours: Strict quiet hours are enforced after 10:30 PM to ensure a peaceful study and sleep environment.
  • Electrical Appliances: High-power electrical appliances like heaters, kettles, and induction stoves are strictly forbidden in rooms.
  • Outpasses: Leaving the campus requires an approved outpass through the AUMS portal. Parents will be contacted for home passes.
  • Cleanliness: Students are responsible for maintaining the cleanliness of their rooms. Garbage must be disposed of in designated bins.
